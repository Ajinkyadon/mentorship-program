## What is This Program?

Ye ek **complete mentorship + training program** hai jo specially design kiya gaya hai:

- CSE/IT students ke liye jo confuse hain "kya seekhein?"
- Tier 2/Tier 3 college ke students ke liye
- Freshers jinhe coding se darr lagta hai
- Students jo placements crack karna chahte hain
- Jo log tutorials dekhte hain but projects nahi bana paate
- Jinhe lagta hai "I'm not good enough"

Yahan theory se zyada **practice** pe focus hai. Real projects, real interview prep, real developer habits.

---

## Program Vision

> **Ek aisa program jo Indian freshers ko industry-ready developers banaye — confidence ke saath, confusion ke bina.**

### 4 Pillars of This Program

```
1. SAMJHO          → Concepts clearly samajhna, sirf yaad nahi karna
2. BANAO           → Real projects banana jo resume pe dikh sake
3. PRACTICE KARO   → Daily coding habits build karna
4. CRACK KARO      → Interviews, placements, aur jobs
```

### Who Is This For?

✅ Final year CSE/IT students  
✅ Students with 0-1 year experience  
✅ Tier 2/Tier 3 college students  
✅ Students preparing for campus placements  
✅ Students who feel "I don't know where to start"  
✅ Students who watch tutorials but can't build anything alone  

### Who Is This NOT For?

❌ Already experienced developers (2+ years)  
❌ Students looking for shortcuts without effort  
❌ People who won't practice daily

## 3-Month Roadmap (Placement Fast Track)

```
Month 1 — Foundation
├── Week 1–2: Mindset + Developer fundamentals
├── Week 3–4: HTML/CSS/JS basics + first project

Month 2 — Building
├── Week 5–6: JavaScript deep dive + DOM + APIs
├── Week 7–8: Backend basics + Database basics
└── Week 8: Full project (To-Do / Auth App)

Month 3 — Interview Ready
├── Week 9–10: DSA + Problem solving
├── Week 11–12: CS fundamentals (OS, DBMS, CN, OOPs)
└── Week 13: Mock interviews + Resume polish
```

## FAQs

**Q: Mujhe coding bilkul nahi aati, kya ye program mere liye hai?**
A: Haan! Ye program zero se start hota hai. Bas willingness to learn chahiye.

**Q: Kaunsi language seekhni chahiye?**
A: Pehle concepts seekho, language baad mein. Program mein JavaScript use hoga examples ke liye, but concepts kisi bhi language mein apply hote hain.

**Q: Kitne ghante daily dene padenge?**
A: Minimum 2 ghante daily. Jitna zyada, utna fast progress.

**Q: Kya service companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro) ke liye bhi kaam aayega?**
A: Bilkul. CS fundamentals, aptitude, aur HR prep sab cover hai.

**Q: Kya product companies (Flipkart, Swiggy, startups) ke liye bhi prepare kar sakta hoon?**
A: Haan — DSA, system design basics, aur projects wali section especially product companies ke liye hai.

**Q: Mera college placement nahi aaya, kya off-campus ke liye bhi kaam aayega?**
A: Haan. LinkedIn optimization, GitHub, aur off-campus application strategy bhi covered hai.

**Q: Kya guarantee hai ki job milegi?**
A: Koi guarantee nahi — lekin jo consistently follow karega, uske paas real skills, real projects, aur real confidence hogi. Baaki aap par hai.
